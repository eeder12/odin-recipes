Description

Simple Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda/li>
  • Fine salt
  • 1 1/2 sticks unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • One 12-ounce bag semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat to 375 degrees F
  2. Whisk together the flour, baking soda and 1 teaspoon salt in a large bowl.
  3. Beat the Butter and both sugars: Beat on medium-high speed in the bowl until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time while beating to incorporate eggs. Beat in the vanilla. Add in the flour mixture and beat until incorporated. Stir in the chocolate chips
  4. Scoop 12 tablespoons of dough about 2 inches apart on the baking shoot Bake until golden, roughly 12 to 15 minutes, bring out the cookies and let them cool down
  5. Eat!
